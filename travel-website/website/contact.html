Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for Mount Rinjani treks and during peak season (July-September). However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests.

What's included in your tour packages?

Our packages typically include transportation, professional guides, entrance fees, equipment rental, and meals as specified. Accommodation can be arranged upon request.

Do you offer transportation from the airport?

Yes, we provide airport transfers from Lombok International Airport to your accommodation and tour starting points. This can be arranged when booking.

What's the best time to visit Lombok?

The dry season (April-October) offers the best weather for most activities. For volcano trekking, avoid the rainy season (November-March) when trails can be dangerous.

Are your tours suitable for beginners?

We offer activities for all fitness levels. Our guides will assess your abilities and recommend appropriate adventures. We have easy beach tours to challenging multi-day treks.

What should I bring for activities?

We provide most equipment, but you should bring comfortable clothing, sunscreen, personal medications, and appropriate footwear. We'll send a detailed packing list upon booking.
