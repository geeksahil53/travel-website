Photography Tips for Lombok

Golden Hour Magic

The best lighting occurs during sunrise and sunset. Plan your photography sessions around these times for warm, dramatic lighting that enhances natural beauty.

Respect Local Culture

Always ask permission before photographing people, especially in traditional villages. Many locals appreciate a small donation for portraits.

Protect Your Gear

Bring waterproof bags for beach and waterfall photography. The tropical climate can be humid, so silica gel packets help prevent lens fogging.

Capture Local Life

Visit local markets, fishing villages, and craft workshops early in the morning for authentic scenes of daily Sasak life and traditional activities.
