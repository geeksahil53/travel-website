Popular Adventure Activities

Experience the best of what Lombok has to offer with these must-do adventures

Most Popular
Challenging

Mount Rinjani Volcano Trekking

2-4 days April - November 4-12 people

Embark on Indonesia's most spectacular volcano trek to the summit of Mount Rinjani (3,726m). This challenging multi-day adventure takes you through diverse ecosystems, past the stunning Segara Anak crater lake, and rewards you with breathtaking sunrise views from the summit.

Trek Highlights:

  • Summit sunrise at 3,726 meters
  • Beautiful Segara Anak crater lake
  • Natural hot springs for relaxation
  • Panoramic views of Lombok and Bali
  • Camping under pristine mountain skies
Difficulty: Challenging (good fitness required)
Equipment: All camping gear provided
Includes: Guide, porter, meals, camping equipment
Easy

Gili Islands Snorkeling & Diving

Half/Full day Year-round 2-20 people

Explore the underwater paradise of the Gili Islands with their pristine coral reefs, colorful tropical fish, and friendly sea turtles. The crystal-clear waters offer excellent visibility for both beginners and experienced divers.

Marine Life Highlights:

  • Swimming with sea turtles
  • Vibrant coral reef ecosystems
  • Excellent underwater visibility
  • PADI certification courses available
Difficulty: Easy to Moderate
Equipment: Snorkel gear and wetsuits provided
Includes: Boat transfer, guide, equipment, lunch
Moderate

Waterfall Discovery Hikes

3-6 hours Year-round 2-15 people

Trek through lush tropical rainforests to discover Lombok's most spectacular waterfalls. These adventures combine moderate hiking with refreshing swims in pristine natural pools surrounded by verdant jungle.

Waterfall Adventures:

  • Swimming in natural pools
  • Tropical rainforest trekking
  • Stunning photography opportunities
  • Cool mountain water refreshment
Featured Waterfalls: Tiu Kelep,Sendang Gile,Benang Stokel
Includes: Local guide, entrance fees, lunch
Moderate

Surfing Adventures

2-4 hours April - October 1-8 people

Ride world-class waves along Lombok's stunning southern coastline. From beginner-friendly beach breaks to challenging reef breaks, Lombok offers surfing experiences for all skill levels in a beautiful tropical setting.

Surf Spots:

  • Beginner lessons at Kuta Beach
  • Advanced breaks at Desert Point
  • Consistent waves year-round
  • Less crowded than Bali
Equipment: Surfboards and wetsuits provided
Includes: Instructor, board rental, safety briefing
Easy

Island Hopping Tours

Full day Year-round 6-25 people

Explore multiple pristine islands around Lombok on traditional boats. Visit secluded beaches, snorkel in different locations, and experience the unique character of each island paradise.

Island Tour Highlights:

  • Traditional outrigger boat transport
  • Multiple pristine beach stops
  • Snorkeling at different coral reefs
  • Fresh seafood lunch on the beach
Includes: Boat transfer, snorkel gear, lunch, guide
Islands: Gili Air, Gili Meno, Gili Trawangan
Easy

Cultural Village Tours

3-5 hours Year-round 2-12 people

Immerse yourself in authentic Sasak culture by visiting traditional villages where ancient customs and crafts are preserved. Meet local artisans, learn traditional skills, and gain insight into Indonesia's rich cultural heritage.

Cultural Experiences:

  • Traditional architecture tours
  • Artisan craft demonstrations
  • Local cooking experiences
  • Traditional music and dance
Villages: Sade, Ende, Sukarara, Banyumulek
Includes: Local guide, village donations, transportation

Plan Your Perfect Adventure

To make the most of your Lombok adventure, consider your fitness level, interests, and travel dates when selecting activities. Our local guides are experts in creating personalized itineraries that combine multiple activities for the ultimate Indonesian experience.

Fitness Level

Choose activities that match your physical capabilities. We offer options from easy beach relaxation to challenging multi-day treks.

Seasonal Considerations

Some activities are seasonal. Plan volcano treks during dry season (April-November) for best conditions.

Equipment & Preparation

Most equipment is provided, but bring appropriate clothing, sunscreen, and personal items for comfort.

Group Options

Join group tours for socializing or book private experiences for personalized attention and flexibility.

Safety & Preparation

Professional Guides

All activities are led by certified local guides with extensive knowledge of safety procedures and emergency protocols.

Safety Equipment

Professional safety equipment is provided for all activities, including life jackets, helmets, and first aid supplies.

Emergency Communication

Guides carry emergency communication devices and have established protocols for various emergency situations.

Health Requirements

Inform guides of any medical conditions. Basic fitness is required for trekking activities, swimming ability for water sports.
