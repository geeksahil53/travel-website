Must-Visit Places in Lombok

From pristine beaches to towering volcanos, Lombok offers diverse landscapes that will take your breath away

Featured

Gili Islands

Northwest of Lombok 30 min by boat

Three tropical paradise islands offering pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life. Gili Trawangan is known for its nightlife, Gili Air for its laid-back atmosphere, and Gili Meno for romantic getaways. Perfect for snorkeling, diving, and enjoying stunning sunsets.

Highlights:

  • World-class snorkeling and diving
  • No motorized vehicles - bicycles and horse carts only
  • Beach bars and sunset viewing spots
  • Swimming with sea turtles
Excellent for water sports

Mount Rinjani

Central Lombok 3,726m elevation

Indonesia's second-highest volcano and a sacred mountain for the Sasak people. The challenging multi-day trek to the summit rewards adventurers with breathtaking views of the crater lake (Segara Anak), hot springs, and unforgettable sunrise experiences above the clouds.

Highlights:

  • Spectacular sunrise views from the summit
  • Beautiful crater lake Segara Anak
  • Natural hot springs for relaxation
  • Multi-day trekking adventure
Challenging but rewarding

Sekotong Peninsula

Southwest Lombok 1.5 hours from Mataram

A hidden gem featuring secluded beaches, dramatic cliffs, and traditional fishing villages. The area offers pristine coastlines with fewer crowds, making it perfect for those seeking tranquility and authentic local experiences away from tourist hotspots.

Highlights:

  • Secluded pristine beaches
  • Dramatic coastal cliff views
  • Traditional fishing villages
  • Less crowded than other areas
Perfect for relaxation

Tiu Kelep Waterfall

North Lombok 45 min jungle trek

A spectacular multi-tiered waterfall hidden deep in the tropical rainforest near Mount Rinjani. The adventurous jungle trek through lush vegetation leads to this magnificent 42-meter cascade, where visitors can swim in refreshing natural pools surrounded by pristine nature.

Highlights:

  • Swimming in natural pools
  • Lush tropical rainforest trek
  • Spectacular photography opportunities
  • Cool, refreshing mountain water
Nature lover's paradise

Kuta Lombok

South Lombok 1 hour from airport

Not to be confused with Kuta Bali, this southern coastal gem offers world-class surfing, powdery white sand beaches, and stunning turquoise waters. The area maintains its authentic charm while providing excellent facilities for beach lovers and surf enthusiasts.

Highlights:

  • World-class surfing spots
  • Powdery white sand beaches
  • Excellent snorkeling opportunities
  • Beachside restaurants and cafes
Perfect for surfing

Sendang Gile Waterfall

North Lombok 15 min easy walk

An easily accessible waterfall that serves as the gateway to Tiu Kelep. This beautiful 31-meter cascade offers a more relaxed experience for families and those who prefer shorter hikes. The surrounding area features traditional Sasak villages and stunning mountain views.

Highlights:

  • Family-friendly easy access
  • Traditional Sasak village nearby
  • Beautiful photography spots
  • Gateway to Tiu Kelep waterfall
Great for families

Planning Your Lombok Adventure

Best Time to Visit

Visit during dry season (April-October) for the best weather. Mount Rinjani treks are best avoided during rainy season (November-March).

Getting Around

Rent a scooter for flexibility, hire a driver for comfort, or join organized tours for hassle-free exploration of multiple destinations.

What to Pack

Bring reef-safe sunscreen, comfortable hiking shoes, quick-dry clothing, and a waterproof bag for electronics.

Local Respect

Respect local customs, dress modestly when visiting villages, and always ask permission before photographing people.
